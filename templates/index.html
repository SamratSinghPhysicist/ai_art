Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI thumbnail generator work?

Our AI thumbnail generator uses advanced machine learning algorithms to create professional YouTube thumbnails based on your video description. The AI understands the context of your content and generates visually appealing images optimized for higher click-through rates.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our basic thumbnail generation service is completely free to use. Create as many thumbnails as you need without any cost. For advanced features and higher resolution images, we offer premium plans.

Can I customize the generated thumbnails?

Absolutely! You can customize your thumbnails by providing a reference image to guide the AI's style and composition. Additionally, you can download the generated thumbnails and further edit them in your preferred image editing software.

What image resolution do I get?

All thumbnails are generated at YouTube's recommended resolution of 1280x720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio). This ensures your thumbnails look crisp and professional across all devices and screen sizes.

How do I integrate the thumbnail generator with my workflow?

For content creators who need automated solutions, we offer a simple API that allows you to generate thumbnails programmatically. Check our API documentation for details on how to integrate it into your content workflow.
