Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to generate a video?

Video generation typically takes 5-7 minutes depending on the complexity of your prompt and current server load. You can monitor the progress in real-time with our status updates.

What video formats are supported?

All generated videos are provided in MP4 format with H.264 encoding, ensuring compatibility with all modern browsers and devices. Videos are optimized for web playback and sharing.

Can I use the generated videos commercially?

Yes, you have full rights to use the generated videos for both personal and commercial purposes. This includes social media content, marketing materials, presentations, and any other creative projects.

What makes a good video prompt?

Effective prompts are descriptive and specific. Include details about the scene, lighting, movement, and style. For example: "A serene lake at sunset with gentle ripples and birds flying overhead" works better than just "lake video".
